Frequently asked questions

Practical answers about our products, cart, checkout, PayPal, shipping inquiries and how to care for the pieces.

Most pieces in the catalogue are produced in small batches. After you submit an Order First request or complete PayPal checkout, our team confirms current stock and lead times for each item. For larger items (vanities, shower systems) lead times are typically 2–6 weeks.

Our faucets, mixers and shower systems use solid brass cores with living-finish copper, smoked bronze or brushed graphite surfaces — no plated finishes that flake over time. Cartridges are serviceable so the fitting can be kept in use for many years.

Wipe surfaces with a soft cloth and warm water — avoid acidic or abrasive cleaners. Living-finish copper and brass develop a soft patina with use, which is intended. Stone and ceramic basins should be wiped after use to avoid limescale rings.
