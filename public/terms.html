1. Purpose of this website

The ALFANAR website is operated by ALFANAR CONSTRUCTION SINGLE MEMBER PC as a frontend product introduction and inquiry website for the studio's catalogue of interior furniture and bathroom fittings. It allows visitors to browse pieces, build a cart, request information and either submit an order request or complete a PayPal payment.

2. Product information

Product names, descriptions, photographs, specifications and prices are provided in good faith for demonstration and catalogue purposes. Dimensions, finishes and availability may need to be confirmed by the studio before an order is fulfilled. Photography may show patina, lighting and styling that vary from the delivered piece.

3. Order First requests

Selecting Order First at checkout submits an order request. No payment is taken at the time of submission. Our team will contact you to confirm product availability, finish selections, shipping arrangements and the final invoice. The order is considered placed only once both parties have confirmed in writing.

4. PayPal payments

Selecting PayPal at checkout processes the payment through PayPal's official checkout interface, embedded via the PayPal JavaScript SDK. PayPal login and any required identity steps are performed entirely on PayPal's official screens. ALFANAR does not collect or store your PayPal login.

5. No card data is collected

The website does not collect, process or store card numbers, expiry dates, CVV codes, Mastercard or Visa data, debit card or credit card details. There is no manual card form anywhere on this website.

6. Availability and shipping

Stock levels for handmade and small-batch items can change between site visits. Lead times, shipping costs and customs handling are confirmed at the time of order confirmation. Estimated totals shown in the cart and at checkout are indicative and do not include shipping or applicable taxes unless explicitly stated.

7. Prices

Prices are displayed in USD and represent the studio's list prices at the time of publication. Prices are subject to confirmation by ALFANAR before an invoice is issued, and the studio reserves the right to correct evident errors in price or specification.

8. Cart storage

Your cart is stored in your browser's localStorage under the key carly_cart. Clearing browser data or using a different device will reset the cart. The studio does not receive your cart contents until you complete a checkout or submit a "Ask about selected products" inquiry.

9. Demonstration forms

All forms on this website (contact, checkout, inquiry) are frontend forms. They are intended to capture your message and selection so that the studio can respond. No automated transaction or contract is formed until ALFANAR confirms acceptance directly with you.

10. Product care responsibility

Many ALFANAR pieces use natural materials with living finishes (brass, copper, stone, oak). Care instructions are supplied with each piece. Damage caused by abrasive cleaners, prolonged exposure to standing water or improper installation is not covered under warranty.

11. Limitation of liability

To the extent permitted by Greek law, ALFANAR is not liable for indirect or consequential losses arising from the use of this website. The studio's liability for any confirmed order is limited to the invoiced value of the goods supplied.
