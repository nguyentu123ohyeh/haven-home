A studio practice

Considered objects for the rooms we live in.

From floating vanities and sculpted basins to brass mixers and floor-mounted bath fillers, every piece in the ALFANAR catalogue is selected for its material honesty, its proportions, and the way it ages.

Our practice is small by design. We work closely with foundries, joineries and ceramic studios across Europe to produce a tight collection — never trend-driven, always rooted in interior architecture.

Signature material

Travertine, fumed oak and aged copper.

Our material library is small and disciplined. Honed travertine for tops; fumed European oak for cabinetry; living-finish copper that gathers a soft patina from use. We avoid plated finishes that flake and timber stains that fade — only materials that wear into the room.

Bathroom as room

Fittings that hold a bathroom together.

We treat the bathroom as a room — not a wet zone. Thermostatic shower cores, recessed mixer plates and floor-mounted bath fillers are sized to align cleanly with tiles, niches and vanity surfaces. Installer notes are included with every fitting.
