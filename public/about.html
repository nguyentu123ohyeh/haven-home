About the studio

A studio practice rooted in material, proportion and patience.

ALFANAR designs and curates interior furniture and bathroom fittings for residences, ateliers and small hospitality projects across Europe. Our collection is deliberately small, our materials deliberately honest.

Brand story

Begun in Athens, made for considered interiors.

ALFANAR began as a small workshop sourcing brass fittings for a single architecture studio in Athens. Over fifteen years it grew into a focused practice combining furniture, bathroom fittings and the in-between objects that finish a room — mirrors, shelves, niches, basins.

We do not chase seasonal collections. We add a piece when it earns a place and we keep it for as long as the materials and the makers allow.

Interior furniture

Furniture for the rooms that frame the day.

Consoles in solid walnut, open oak shelving, arched plaster mirrors. We design with traditional joinery and finish in hardwax oils and lime plaster — surfaces that respond to light and use.

Bathroom fittings

Bathrooms detailed like architecture.

Thermostatic shower cores, wall-mounted mixers and floor-standing bath fillers, finished in living-bronze and aged copper. Cartridges are serviceable; flow rates are calibrated; installer notes ship with every fitting.
