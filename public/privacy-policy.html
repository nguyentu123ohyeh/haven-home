Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. About this policy

The ALFANAR website is a frontend product introduction and inquiry website operated by ALFANAR CONSTRUCTION SINGLE MEMBER PC. This policy explains what limited information may be collected when you interact with the website's forms, how it is used, and how payments via PayPal are handled.

2. Information that may be collected

The website itself does not operate a backend database. Information you submit through the contact form or checkout form is used to respond to your inquiry or arrange your order. The forms may collect:

  • Full Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • City / State
  • Shipping address
  • Order note
  • Inquiry message
  • The list of products currently in your cart (when you send a product inquiry or proceed to checkout)

3. How information is used

  • To confirm product availability and prepare an order quotation
  • To arrange shipping and delivery details with you directly
  • To provide customer support after a purchase or installation
  • To answer product, finish or specification questions
  • To process PayPal payments by redirecting you to the official PayPal interface

4. Cookies and local browser storage

The website uses your browser's localStorage to remember two things:

  • Your shopping cart contents (under the key carly_cart), so your selection is preserved between pages and sessions.
  • Your cookie preference (Accept or Decline) so the cookie banner is not shown again.

No third-party analytics, advertising or tracking cookies are used. No backend database is operated by this website.

5. Payments and PayPal

PayPal payments are handled exclusively through the official PayPal JavaScript SDK. The PayPal button opens PayPal's own secure interface. ALFANAR does not collect, see, store or transmit your PayPal email, PayPal password, card number, expiry date, CVV or any other payment credentials.

6. What we do not collect

  • No PayPal login credentials
  • No card number, expiry, CVV or other card data
  • No tracking identifiers for advertising
  • No location data beyond what you voluntarily provide in the form

7. Your rights

You may request access, correction or deletion of any personal information you have shared with us by writing to info@alfanar-construction.gr. You may clear your locally-stored cart at any time by removing items in the Cart page or clearing your browser storage.
